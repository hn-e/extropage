## Overview

Developed mobile apps for android and ios operating system 
for people to connect and organize parties with people from near by locations /
planning a trip to outstation with attractive UI

## Key Features

- **Build Real world Connections**: Connect with real world people
- **Create Parties & Chat with members**: With Extrowurts app it is very easy to create parties on any locations and invite people
- **Award Party members**: Extrowurts app allows users to award the party members which are then displayed on party members profile after party is completed
- **See profiles of people across app**: you can visit the profile of members and even see the profile for the people requesting to join the party
